About Me

Hi, I’m Alex 👋

I created NeedThingsDone because finding trustworthy businesses shouldn’t be difficult. Whether someone needs a plumber, roofer, dog walker, computer technician, or simply advice from the community, finding the right help should be simple.

My goal is to build a place where Canadians can discover businesses, support local individuals, find deals, ask questions, and connect with their communities.
